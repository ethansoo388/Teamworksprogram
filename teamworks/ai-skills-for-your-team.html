COURSE MODULES

What You'll Learn

A structured, hands-on journey from AI fundamentals to real-world application — designed for teams who want results, not theory.

01

AI Basics for Busy Teams

Get hands-on from day one

Understand what AI can and cannot do at work. Learn where AI adds real value, where it doesn't, and experience your first hands-on interaction with AI in a safe, guided way.

02

Prompting That Actually Works

Master the art of AI conversation

Learn how to communicate with AI clearly and effectively. Participants are guided step-by-step through simple prompt structures to get better, more useful outputs.

03

AI for Everyday Work

Use AI for real tasks immediately

Apply AI to common workplace tasks such as emails, reports, summaries, research, and planning through group-based activities focused on real work scenarios.

04

The "Role-Based Deep Dives" with AI (Specialist Tracks)

Tailored to your team's needs

Explore practical AI use cases for different teams including Marketing, Operations, HR, IT, and Sales. Learn how AI supports each role without replacing human judgement.

05

Safe and Responsible AI at Work

Build trust and confidence

Understand common AI risks such as data privacy, hallucinations, and over-reliance. Learn how to use AI responsibly through demonstrations using simulated data.

06

Final AI Challenge: End-to-End Simulation

Apply everything you've learned

Teams take on realistic departmental scenarios and use AI tools end-to-end to solve problems, improve outputs, and present results based on everything learned during the course.
