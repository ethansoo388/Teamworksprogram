Common Questions

Everything you need to know about designing like Apple.

Do I need design experience to attend?

No design experience required. We teach you the principles and give you practical tools you can use immediately.

Will this work for non-tech products?

Absolutely! Apple's design principles apply to any product or service—physical, digital, or hybrid. We'll show you how.

What tools or software do we use?

We use simple, accessible tools like paper prototypes, Figma basics, and collaboration frameworks. No expensive software required.

How hands-on is this workshop?

Very hands-on! 70% practical exercises and real case studies. You'll leave with actionable frameworks and templates.

Can I bring my team?

Yes! We recommend bringing 2-5 team members. Team cohesion and shared learning lead to better implementation.
