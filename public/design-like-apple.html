Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Design Like Apple.

Do I need design experience to attend?

No. This course is designed for anyone who builds products or services for customers. We teach you the principles and frameworks from scratch. The only requirement is curiosity and a willingness to learn.

Is this just theory or will I learn practical skills?

This is a hands-on workshop. You'll work on real challenges, practice customer interviews, create journey maps, and build prototypes. You leave with tools and templates you can use immediately.

What materials do I need to bring?

Just bring yourself and an open mind. We provide all materials, workbooks, templates, and tools. You'll also receive digital resources to use after the workshop.

Can I bring my team?

Absolutely! We encourage teams to attend together. When multiple team members learn the same frameworks, it's easier to implement Design Thinking back at your office. We offer group discounts for teams of 5 or more.

How is this different from other design thinking workshops?

We focus on practical application over theory. Everything is taught through real examples and hands-on exercises. You'll learn from facilitators who've actually used these methods to build successful products, not just academics who teach theory.
